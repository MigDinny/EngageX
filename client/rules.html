RULES

The objective of this game is to be the first player to achieve 250 XP points.


You will have to move at the speed of the song. Each beat of the song is an action you can do. The song is 128 bpm, meaning you can do an action each 0.5 seconds. You can use WASD to move. There are 3 main abilities:

  • <Q> Harvest: Eat grass in current cell for +40 HP
  • <W> Sow: Grow grass in adjacent cells for -33% HP
  • <E> Save XP: Lose 50%HP.Lost HP converted into XP

    • If you are in the same cell as another player, you enter battle mode and gain new abiliteis:

  • <Q> Fight: Oponent loses 25HP
  • <W> Steal: 50% chance to steal 25% of oponnents XP. If fails XP gains 25% of your XP
  • <E> Share: Both players share 50% of their HP
  • <R> Flee: Lose 33%HP. Move 3 squares away

    • If you lose all your HP you die, meaning you cannot move until the game ends.



    You now know the rules. Good luck and have fun!
